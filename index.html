    Lighting is a key element in photography that affects mood and photo quality. Basic techniques include golden hour for soft natural light, or using softboxes for studio setups. Explore backlighting for dramatic silhouettes or fill light to avoid harsh shadows. Use reflectors to control light. Example: In portraits, side lighting creates dimension. With practice, you can master lighting for genres like fashion or product photography, resulting in more vibrant and professional photos.
